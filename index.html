# Graphics Programming
[Home](/) • [Resources](/resources) • [Showcase](/showcase)

Graphics Programming is an online community of graphics programmers of all backgrounds and experience levels. The community is primarily based through Discord. In the community, the spread of knowledge of APIs, rendering techniques, and more, is promoted in order to push the boundaries of what's possible. For the spread of knowledge, members of the community have pooled together knowledge in a central location at our [resources page](/resources). Many members of the community have projects, from simple renderers, to game engines, to widely used APIs. In addition, there is currently a community vulkan accelerated ray tracer in development.
